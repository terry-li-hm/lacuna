# Liquidity Reporting Procedure

## Purpose
Provide procedures for Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) reporting, disclosure, and regulatory submission.

## Scope
Applies to Treasury and Regulatory Reporting teams for monthly and quarterly LCR reports.

## Key Requirements
- Calculate LCR daily; report monthly with full reconciliation.
- Disclose LCR metrics in regulatory filings on prescribed timelines.
- Maintain supporting workpapers and approval logs for audit purposes.

## Governance
Treasury owns calculations; Regulatory Reporting owns submissions; Risk reviews quarterly.
