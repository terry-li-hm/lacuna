# Risk Governance Standard

## Purpose
Establish governance standards for model risk, policy approvals, and regulatory change oversight.

## Scope
Applies to all business units and subsidiaries operating regulated banking activities.

## Key Requirements
- Maintain a centralized register of regulatory changes with ownership and due dates.
- Use an approval workflow for material policy updates.
- Keep audit trails for regulatory change decisions and evidence of implementation.

## Governance
Second line of defense owns standards; First line implements; Third line audits annually.
