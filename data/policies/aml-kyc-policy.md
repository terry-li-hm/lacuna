# AML/KYC Policy

## Purpose
Define customer due diligence (CDD), enhanced due diligence (EDD), and ongoing monitoring requirements to prevent money laundering and terrorist financing risks.

## Scope
Applies to all customer onboarding and periodic review processes for retail and corporate clients.

## Key Requirements
- Conduct CDD at onboarding and refresh at least annually for high-risk customers.
- Apply EDD for PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions, and complex ownership structures.
- Maintain transaction monitoring rules aligned to risk appetite and regulatory thresholds.
- Retain KYC records and screening evidence for 7 years.

## Governance
Compliance owns policy updates; Operations executes control procedures; Internal Audit reviews annually.
