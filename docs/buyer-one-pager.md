# RegAtlas — Buyer One‑Pager

## What it is
RegAtlas is a regulatory intelligence and change‑management platform for financial institutions. It turns regulatory text into structured obligations, tracks changes across jurisdictions, and creates audit‑ready workflows for assessment and remediation.

## Why it matters
Compliance teams are drowning in updates, spreadsheets, and manual assessment. RegAtlas reduces cycle time, improves traceability, and provides a defensible audit trail.

## Core capabilities
- **Horizon scanning** (RSS/Atom) → auto‑create change items
- **Obligation extraction** → structured requirements with evidence
- **Change register** → owners, due dates, approvals, impact assessments
- **Risk scoring & escalation** → prioritize critical changes
- **Policy library** → map changes to internal policies and controls
- **Evidence management** → upload and link audit artifacts
- **GenAI assist** → draft impact summaries and map relevant obligations
- **Exports & integrations** → CSV + JSON for downstream GRC systems

## Outcomes you can expect
- Faster triage and assessment of regulatory changes
- Better coverage across jurisdictions and subsidiaries
- Clear audit trail and stronger regulatory defensibility
- Reduced manual work for compliance teams

## Deployment options

- **AWS** (ECS/Fargate scaffold)
